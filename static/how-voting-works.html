How Voting Works

Your complete guide to secure digital voting

Understanding Our Secure Voting System

Digi-Vote uses state-of-the-art technology to ensure your vote is secure, private, and accurately counted. This guide explains how our system works from registration to results.

Security Measures

Digi-Vote employs multiple layers of security to protect the integrity of every election.

Blockchain Technology

Every vote is recorded on a decentralized blockchain, creating an immutable record that cannot be altered or deleted. How it works Votes are encrypted and stored as blocks in a chain. Each block contains a timestamp and link to the previous block, making it impossible to alter past records without detection.

Tamper Resistance 99.99%

End-to-end Encryption

Your vote is encrypted from the moment you cast it until it's counted, ensuring only authorized systems can access it. How it works We use 256-bit AES encryption combined with RSA public-key cryptography to ensure that only the vote counting system can decrypt your ballot.

Data Protection 100%

Two-factor Authentication

Multiple verification steps ensure only eligible voters can access the system and cast their vote. How it works After entering your credentials, a one-time code is sent to your registered mobile device or email to verify your identity before you can access the voting system.

Identity Verification 99.7%

Audit Trail

A complete record of all voting activity is maintained while preserving voter anonymity. How it works The system records timestamps and actions without linking to personal identifiers, allowing for verification of process integrity without compromising voter privacy.

Transparency 98.5%

Security Certifications

Our system has been certified by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and undergoes regular security audits by independent cybersecurity firms.

Step-by-Step Voting Process

Follow these steps to cast your vote securely in any Digi-Vote election.

1

Login & Authentication

Estimated time: 1 min

Access the Digi-Vote platform using your registered credentials. You'll need your Voter ID and password to begin.

Enter your Voter ID and password on the login screen
Complete the two-factor authentication by entering the code sent to your mobile device
Review and accept the terms and conditions for the voting process
2

Ballot Selection

Estimated time: 2-5 mins

View available elections and select the one you wish to participate in. You'll see all candidates or proposals with detailed information.

Browse the list of active elections on your dashboard
Click on an election to view the ballot with candidates or proposals
Review candidate profiles or proposal details before making your selection
3

Cast Your Vote

Estimated time: 1-2 mins

Make your selection by clicking on your preferred candidate or option. You can review your choice before final submission.

Select your preferred candidate or option by clicking on their name or the radio button
For multiple-selection ballots, you can choose up to the maximum allowed options
Review your selection on the confirmation screen before submitting

Once submitted, your vote cannot be changed. Take your time to make your selection.

4

Verification & Confirmation

Estimated time: 1 min

After submitting your vote, you'll receive a confirmation with a unique receipt number that you can use to verify your vote was counted.

Receive an on-screen confirmation that your vote was successfully recorded
Note your unique receipt number for future reference
Receive an email confirmation with your receipt number (optional)
5

Results & Verification

Available after election closes

Once the election closes, results are tallied and published. You can verify your vote was included in the final count using your receipt number.

View election results on the results page after voting closes
Use the verification tool to confirm your vote was included in the final tally
Access detailed analytics and breakdowns of the voting results

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Digi-Vote system and process.

Is my vote really anonymous?

Yes, your vote is completely anonymous. While the system verifies your identity to ensure you're eligible to vote, once your ballot is submitted, it's separated from your personal information. The blockchain records only encrypted votes that cannot be traced back to individual voters. Even system administrators cannot link votes to specific voters.

What happens if I lose my internet connection while voting?

The Digi-Vote system is designed to handle connection interruptions. If you lose your connection during the voting process but before submitting your ballot, your selections will be saved temporarily on your device. When you reconnect and log back in, you can continue from where you left off. However, if the connection is lost during the actual submission process, your vote might not be recorded, and you'll need to start over.

Can I change my vote after submitting it?

No, once your vote is submitted and confirmed, it cannot be changed or retracted. This is why we include a review step before final submission, allowing you to verify your selections. This immutability is a key security feature of the blockchain technology that powers our system, ensuring votes cannot be altered after they're cast.

What devices can I use to vote?

Digi-Vote is compatible with most modern devices and browsers. You can vote using a desktop computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone. We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge browsers for the best experience. The system is responsive and will adapt to your screen size, though a larger screen provides a better viewing experience for some elections with complex ballots.

How do I know my vote was counted correctly?

After submitting your vote, you'll receive a unique receipt ID and QR code. Once the election closes, you can use these to verify that your vote was included in the final tally through our verification portal. This verification process confirms your vote was recorded and counted without revealing your specific choices, maintaining your privacy while ensuring transparency.

What if I need assistance during the voting process?

Help is available throughout the voting process. You can use the "Help" button on any screen to access context-specific guidance. For more assistance, our support team is available via live chat during voting hours, or you can call our support hotline at +1 (555) 987-6543. If you have a disability that requires special accommodation, please contact our accessibility team before the election.

Has the system ever been hacked?

No, the Digi-Vote system has never been successfully hacked. We employ multiple layers of security and conduct regular security audits and penetration testing by independent cybersecurity firms. Our blockchain technology makes the system inherently resistant to tampering. We also maintain a bug bounty program where security researchers can responsibly disclose vulnerabilities, helping us continuously improve our security posture.

What happens to my data after the election?

Your anonymized vote remains permanently on the blockchain for verification and audit purposes. However, personal identification data used for authentication is stored separately and is retained only as long as required by applicable regulations. After this period, personal data is securely deleted while maintaining the integrity of the anonymous voting record.
