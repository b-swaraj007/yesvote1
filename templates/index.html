About Our Project

Digi-Vote is a cutting-edge electronic voting platform developed by AIML students Of Dr. D. Y. Patil College of engineering and technology, kolhapur that integrates facial recognition technology with secure blockchain-based voting systems to ensure transparent, tamper-proof elections.

Our system addresses critical challenges in traditional voting methods by eliminating voter fraud, reducing administrative costs, and making the voting process more accessible while maintaining the highest standards of security and privacy.

Facial Recognition

Advanced biometric verification ensures one-person-one-vote integrity and prevents identity fraud.

Blockchain Security

Immutable ledger technology ensures votes cannot be altered, deleted, or manipulated once cast.

Accessibility

Remote voting capabilities with multi-language support makes democracy more inclusive.

Real-time Analytics

Instant vote counting and result verification with comprehensive audit trails.
